We Offer Four Types of Student Programs

Overview

Catchup Math is for students in grades 7 and above, but our Programs will "drill down" to review material taught in elementary school as necessary. We offer Subject Proficiency Programs, Graduation Test Prep Programs, Chapter-Based Programs and Custom (Teacher-Created) Programs.

Subject Proficiency

Each Subject Proficiency Program reviews a full subject in six sections. Students advance from section to section by taking and passing quizzes. Our Subject Proficiency Programs are Essentials (covering grades 6-7 Math), Pre-Algebra, Algebra 1, Geometry, and Algebra 2. Teachers may enroll students in specific Proficiency Programs or have them auto-enroll via a placement test.

Graduation Test Prep

Students may prepare for their high school exit exam with one of our Graduation Test Prep Programs. These programs typically cover the material for a specific state, in eight sections. Students advance from section to section as they demonstrate mastery of the material.

Chapter-Based

Teachers can assign material based on scope and sequence correlated with typical textbook chapter topics. Each of the ten or more chapters within our Chapter-Based Programs consists of two sections. Our four Chapter-Based subjects are Pre-Algebra, Algebra 1, Geometry, and Algebra 2.

Custom (Teacher-Created)

Teachers may create their own Programs by selecting among all the lessons contained within Catchup Math. Custom Programs differ from our built-in Programs in that there are no quizzes required to move through the material.
