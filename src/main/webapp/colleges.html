Colleges and Universities

Catchup Math for Your Under-Prepared Students

About half of U.S. college students are unprepared for their first math class. Others may need extra help to get caught up in a class. Catchup Math can rescue these students. Make Catchup Math available at the campus learning center for students to demonstrate readiness for desired courses and/or brush up to the professor's satisfaction. Also, use Catchup Math in lieu of a textbook with students paying online.

Courses

We offer College Basic Math and and College Elementary Algebra Proficiency Programs. We also offer Pre-Algebra and Geometry as both proficiency programs and chapter-based programs (see our Programs page for details).

Faculty Flexibility and Convenience

Setting up students and monitoring progress is faculty friendly! You can create custom lesson sequences and quizzes from our complete collection of typical textbook chapter and section topics. Thus, Catchup Math can be used as a customized, highly engaging, multi-modal, multi-lingual study guide or even as a full-course online textbook!

Free 60-day Pilots

Explore the easy-to-use reporting features and try with all your students for 60 days, free! Sign up here or see our Contact page to request more information. You'll quickly see how easy-to-use and effective Catchup Math is for both students and faculty.
