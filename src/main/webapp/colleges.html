Catchup Math for Your Under-prepared College Students

Many students arrive at college unprepared for their first math class, or need extra help to get caught up in a class. Catchup Math can be recommended or required for these students. Make Catchup Math available at the campus learning center for students to brush up to pass placement tests for desired courses. Please try the free sample Pre-Algebra session on our Home Page.

Free 30-day Pilots

Explore the reporting features and try with students for 30 days, free! Sign up here or email lincoln@hotmath.com with your college name and contact information.

More Information

See our Math Educators page for programs available, research basis, performance studies, and recommended usage. Although we use school course names, the level and material covered correspond 1-1 with typical developmental college math courses.
