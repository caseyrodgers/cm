More Information about Catchup Math

Programs Available

Our programs cover Grades 6-7 Math, Pre-Algebra, Algebra 1, Geometry, Algebra 2, and high school graduation test prep. Foundational skills (e.g., order of operations and fractions) are presented via games, flash cards, and animations. Lessons are accessible in English and Spanish. Faculty can create custom programs. Live online math tutoring is also available.

ELL Students

We follow best practices of math teaching in English for ELL following SDAIE guidelines. For example, we employ redundancy and short phrasing in our tutorials; we utilize image and graphical presentations liberally; our tutorials are self-paced; we support multiple learning modalities with audio-visual, written, and conceptual presentation.
