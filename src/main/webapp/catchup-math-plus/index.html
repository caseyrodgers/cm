Online Teacher Facilitated Credit Recovery

Use Catchup Math Plus!  for Credit Recovery

Here is an exciting new way to make up failed or incomplete grades, online. Students use our proven Catchup Math intervention service from school or home under the direction of an experienced online math teacher. Courses include Essentials (grades 6-7 Math), Pre-Algebra, Algebra 1, Geometry, and Algebra 2.

How Does Catchup Math Plus! Work?

We provide an experienced math Teacher-facilitator to monitor and encourage each student via email and telephone. The Teacher-facilitator answers emailed questions about the subject matter and the use of Catchup Math, holds weekly office hours by telephone, and administers a final exam as required by the school. Upon course completion, the teacher submits a Catchup Math report card to the school.

How Long Does It Take?

Because Catchup Math only re-teaches what students need to learn, using Catchup Math requires an average of 20 login sessions of 30-45 minutes each to pass a course - far less than courses without our adaptive learning technology. The number of sessions will vary depending on ability, learning gaps, and effort. Catchup Math Plus! courses may be completed within days or weeks, but students are required to complete the course within three months.

Pricing and Ordering

Catchup Math Plus! pricing is $299 per student (see our price list and order form). Schools may designate their own teachers to contract with Hotmath, Inc. as the Teacher-facilitator. Courses may be purchased in advance; individual students may then be enrolled as the need arises.
