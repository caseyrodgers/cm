Catchup Math Plus! Teacher-facilitator

Experienced (five years or more) secondary-level teachers of mathematics are engaged by Hotmath, Inc. to perform the functions of the Teacher-facilitator (TF) for Catchup Math Plus!  Each enrolled student is assigned to a TF to see them through to a successful completion as follows:

  • At the beginning of the course, the TF meets with the student by phone or email to discuss the requirements and expectations for passing, to provide encouragement, and assure that the student understands how to log in and use their Catchup Math account.
  • The TF informs the student of the weekly office hours when the TF will be available for telephone (or Skype) consultations.
  • The TF responds daily to emailed questions, and decides whether to recommend that the student engage a tutor if that seems necessary.
  • The TF monitors the student use of the Catchup Math account to assure that the student is making sufficient progress to complete the course on schedule. The TF may also review the handwritten online whiteboard submissions to provide qualitative feedback.
  • When the student has passed all six sections of the course, the TF conducts a final exam (possibly proctored, depending on the requirement of the school).
  • The TF uses the submitted whiteboard work and/or other communications to assure that the course work is being performed by the enrolled student.
  • Upon completion of the course, the TF sends a copy of the Catchup Math report card and a final exam grade to the designated school official, the student, and the parent/guardian.

Schools may request their own teachers to be designated as the TF for their students, and in this case the TF will be paid by Hotmath, Inc.

Math teachers may inquire about a Teacher-facilitator position by emailing admin@hotmath.com with Teacher-facilitator Inquiry in the subject line.
