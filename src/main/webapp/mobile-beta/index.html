Catchup Math Mobile Beta Program

Welcome to the beta program for the new Catchup Math app. Please document any errors, bugs or omissions using the Feedback button on the About page, accessible via any of the local menus. You can also send more detailed feedback to support@catchupmath.com.

There are six Catchup Math Review Programs: Foundations, Essentials, Pre-Algebra, Algebra 1, Geometry, and Algebra 2.

You start the app with our placement test and then are assigned to our lowest Review Program where you may have learning gaps.

Android

If you have an Android phone or tablet, click the following button from your phone. This will lead to a download page for the Android app.

Ios - IPhone, IPad or IPad Pro

In order to participate in the beta test for the iOS, we will need to get your device's UDID. It's not too hard to get the UDID, if you follow these steps:

  • Launch iTunes and connect your iPhone (or iPad).
  • In the right pane, locate the information about your iPhone, including its name, capacity, software version, serial number, and phone number.
  • Reveal the Identifier by clicking on Serial Number.
  • Copy the Identifier to your clipboard by choosing Edit / Copy.

Then, paste it into an email to support@catchupmath.com, and we will send you the access and installation information you will need to begin.
