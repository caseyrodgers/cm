About

Catchup Math Is for At-risk Students

Catchup Math is for students who are behind in math, or who are at risk of falling behind. Catchup Math may be licensed by middle schools, high schools, colleges, or individual students and parents. Catchup Math incorporates award-winning, research-based, performance-proven content used at Hotmath.com. For educator information on Catchup Math see our Math Educators page.

Catchup Math Is a Service of Hotmath, Inc

Hotmath, Inc. has helped students succeed in math since 2000. Our flagship website, Hotmath.com, displays step-by-step answers for the actual, odd-numbered homework problems in over 300 textbooks from middle school math through college algebra and calculus.

Hotmath has earned the respect of the education community from coast to coast. In addition to thousands of schools and millions of students served, we are proud to call major education companies our partners. Please visit these Hotmath pages for more information: About, Teacher Testimonials, Press Room, Awards, Districts, Educators, and Company Background.
