Catchup Math App Beta Test

Welcome to the beta program for the new Catchup Math app. Please document any errors, bugs or omissions using the Feedback button on the About page, accessible via any of the local menus. You can also send more detailed feedback to support@catchupmath.com.

The app is a personalized approach for students or adults interested in relearning forgotten parts of their middle and high school math up through Geometry and Algebra 2. Quizzes are given followed by lessons, videos, and required practice for targeted math topics. The app is not related to the school and college versions of Catchup Math.

The app will be free with in-app purchases for additional "courses" after the first. If you choose a for-pay course, needless to say we will bypass the purchase procedure!
