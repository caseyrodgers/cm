Guide to Assignments

Assign problems to students (for classwork or homework). When students log in, they can work on any open assignments. Students can show their work on the whiteboard, or you can require work to be submitted separately on paper. Problems will be automatically graded and student answers are available for teacher viewing at any time.

Creating Assignments: Choose any multiple-choice, enter-answer, or whiteboard answer problem from our problem bank. Assignments must be made for all students in a Group. Set a due date and whether to allow late submissions. Include a comment for students (e.g., the lessons covered).

Grading: Enter-answer and multiple-choice problems will be automatically graded. If whiteboard-answer problems are assigned, teachers grade those online. Teachers can change the mark for any problem (e.g., to give half-credit for a problem with the wrong answer but good whiteboard effort.).

Open book: Students can view any Catchup Math lessons, so they can get as much help as they need to do well. As an option, students can be told if their entered answers are correct or not as they work on the assignments

Feedback to students: Students can see their score on assignments after the due date, and teachers can give feedback (in red) using the whiteboard at any time.

Re-using Assignments: You can copy an assignment for re-use within the same group (e.g., from last semester) or from another group.
