• Description: Parallel Programs let students use one or more Catchup Math programs in addition to their Primary program.
  • Student Login: Students log in using their school Login Name (e.g., jfk752) and the name of a Parallel Program (e.g., MrSmith-quiz-7) as their initial password. They will then be asked for their personal password.
  • Status: The status of all students who have logged in to any Parallel Program may be viewed with the Usage button.
  • Student Usage: Students may alternate use of their Primary Program and any Parallel Programs by logging out and back in again, in any order they wish. All work on Primary and Parallel Programs is recorded within Student Detail History in reverse chronological order.
