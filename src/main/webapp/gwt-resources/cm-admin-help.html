General Help

We are here to make sure your experience with Catchup Math is successful!

For training or other assistance with using Catchup Math, you have several options:

  • Training Videos
  • Getting Started Guide

If a student experiences difficulties, please view the Technical Tips training video. For other issues, please visit our Support page. The quickest way for us to help is for you to send a email to support@catchupmath.com with a detailed explanation of the issue. If possible, attach a screenshot and include a good phone number. We'll get back to you as soon as we can!
