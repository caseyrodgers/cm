General Help

We are here to make sure your experience with Catchup Math is successful!

If you are experiencing technical difficulties, often the problem can be solved by deleting Temporary Internet Files in your browser (if you are using Internet Explorer) or clearing your browser's cache (if you are using Firefox or Chrome).

If you still experience difficulties, please visit our Support page. Teachers (not students) may call their Account Manager or 510-524-5525 for immediate assistance during school hours. Or, an email can be sent to support@hotmath.com.

If a student experiences difficulty while using Catchup Math, please instruct the student to click on their Help button, click the Feedback button, enter a brief description of the error, and click OK. Our development team will receive the information and, if needed, make corrections within 48 hours.

For training or other assistance with using Catchup Math, you have several options:

  • Training Videos
  • Getting Started Guide
  • FAQs
  • Contact your Account Manager
