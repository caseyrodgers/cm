Feature Updates - March 10, 2012  <top>

Deleting or Modifying a CP or CQ

Previously, if a Custom Program or Custom Quiz was ever assigned, it could not be modified or deleted. We have added a Delete/Archive button to the Custom feature that lets you archive an in-use CP or CQ. Once Archived, the CP or CQ will be unavailable for future assignment and may be copied to a new version and modified.

Quizzes Now Different Every Time

In addition to switching among alternate quizzes for student re-takes, we are now randomizing the order of the questions each time, in an effort to thwart students who seek work-arounds to learning the material to pass the quizzes.

Feature Updates - February 1, 2012  <top>

Extra Practice Problems

We have removed the hints and steps from the Extra Practice Problems. You can ask your students to submit answers using the whiteboard to assure yourself that they understand the material.

NEXT Button Navigation

The NEXT button now lets students select lessons in any order they wish rather than requiring them to advance linearly.

Two New Built-in Custom Programs

We have added two more built in Custom Programs called Foundations A and Foundations B. These cover selected topics generally covered in grades 3-4 and 5-6 respectively.
