Purchase Catchup Math for a School or College

Catchup Math costs $4.75 per student per year (or less) for a school license of 200 students or more. For pricing details, click here. To order Catchup Math for a school or college, print the order form and submit by mail or FAX.

Purchase Catchup Math for one Student

Catchup Math may be purchased for an individual student with a credit card here. Students will take an Auto-Enrollment test and be placed in our lowest level program where they have learning gaps. Then, students proceed at their own pace as far as they wish. For two students in a family, please purchase using separate email addresses, and then email support@hotmath.com to receive a 50% refund on the second account.
