Math-for-All Program from Catchup Math

Free Community Math Help for Non-students

Schools that purchase Catchup Math for their students may elect to offer Catchup Math to non-students in their local Community at no extra charge to the school or the Community. As Catchup Math is fully online and self-contained, there will be no drain on school or teacher resource.

Easy Setup

Simply create a Self-Registration group in your Catchup Math account called "Community" with the Auto-Enrollment Program. (For instructions, please view our video titled Registering Groups and Classes.) Non-students in this group will not be counted towards your license limit. Simply tell Community members to go to catchupmath.com, enter your school Login Name and use "Community" as the initial password. See next section on publicizing Math-for-All.

Press Information (Sample Press Release)

To local press, radio, and TV: Our school uses an online math instructional program called Catchup Math. The company permits Community members seeking to improve in math to use our school account for free, with no extra charge to the school under their "Math-for-All" program.

Catchup Math is ideal for parents (to better help their children), workers (to increase their value to employers) and drop-outs (to re-engage and prepare to return to school).

Catchup Math assesses the level of math proficiency and then provides engaging lessons and practice to move users forward at their own pace, as far as they want to go, up through Geometry and Algebra 2. Learn more about Catchup Math at CATCHUPMATH.COM. To use this free service, click the Login button, enter the school Login Name <put your school login name here> and enter "Community" as the password. Users will be prompted to create an account and then will begin with a (brief) assessment. For any questions or problems, you will be able to send an email to support@catchupmath.com.
