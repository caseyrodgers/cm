Algebra 1 Proficiency Program

Overview

The Catchup Math Algebra 1 Proficiency Program reviews the course content covered in Algebra 1 textbooks and is divided into six sequential sections. Pre-tests (quizzes) are given for each section, and students are assigned lessons based on incorrect answers. Re-teaching includes text lessons (in both English and Spanish), videos, activities, and practice problems with tutorial solutions. A student takes repeat quizzes and receives lesson-prescriptions until passing each section. Teachers can review student work written on our online whiteboard. Material from earlier courses and sections is often included and even repeated.

Section 1

Adding and Subtracting Fractions, Cross Products, Functions, Greatest Common Factors, Least Common Denominator (LCD), Mixed Numbers, Multiplying a Fraction by an Integer, Percent, Point-Slope Form, Prime Factorization, Reciprocals, Rectangle, Reducing Fractions, Slope, Slope-Intercept Form, Solving Linear Equations, Word Problems.

Section 2

Absolute Value, Combining Like Terms, Cross Products, Difference of Squares, Distributive Property, Factoring, Graphing Inequalities in One Variable, Graphing Linear Equations, Greatest Common Factor of Monomials, Number Line, Parallel and Perpendicular Lines, Parallelogram, Percent Increase and Decrease, Scientific Notation, Simplifying Rational Expressions, Solving Linear Inequalities, Solving One-Step Linear Equations, Solving Systems of Linear Equations, Systems of Linear Inequalities, Word Problems.

Section 3

Adding and Subtracting Polynomials, Arithmetic Sequences, Combining Like Terms, Direct Variation, Distributive Property, Factoring, Inverse Variation, Multiplying Polynomials, Parallel and Perpendicular Lines, Simplifying Radical Expressions, Solving Radical Equations.

Section 4

Combining Like Terms, Compound Inequalities, Discriminant, Graphing Linear Equations, Graphing Quadratic Equations, Midpoint Formula, Multiplying Polynomials, Parallel and Perpendicular Lines, Quadratic Formula, Simplifying Rational Expressions, Slope.

Section 5

Cross Products, Distance Formula, Distributive Property, Dividing by a Fraction, Exponential Functions, Exponents, Functions, Principal Value of a Square Root, Quotient of Powers Property, Reciprocals, Simplifying Radical Expressions, Simplifying Rational Expressions, Solving Linear Equations, Solving Radical Equations, Vertical Line Test.

Section 6

Axis of Symmetry of a Parabola, Box and Whisker Plots, Combining Like Terms, Direct Variation, Graphing Quadratic Equations, Interquartile, Mean, Median and Mode, Principal Value of a Square Root, Probability, Pythagorean Theorem, Quadratic Formula, Reducing Fractions, Scatter Plots, Solving One-Step Linear Equations, Vertex of a Parabola, X-Intercepts of a Parabola.
